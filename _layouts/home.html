How I can help

As a qualified Counsellor, my Counselling service provides individuals with a safe and healthy environment to express their feelings, become aware of your values to understand yourself better and develop alternative perspectives on problematic issues you may be experiencing in your life.

My Counselling approach aims to utilise genuineness, respect, empathy and an understanding of your problems in order to help support you with your problems better and alleviate you from symptoms of psychological and mental distress.
